Operational Framework

Three divisions. One discipline.

KHA Marine operates as three integrated divisions, each engineered for a specific maritime mission profile. Together they cover the full spectrum — from elite leisure composites to industrial offshore logistics and urban water-transport networks.

A single engineering culture underwrites every division: marine materials science, mission-driven hull design, and operational discipline matched to Gulf temperature, salinity, and duty cycles.

Division I

Advanced Fiberglass — composite technology, engineered light.

Elite composite technology built around vacuum-infusion processes that hold optimal resin-to-glass ratios. The result: unmatched structural integrity, significant weight reduction over traditional layup, and hulls that survive Gulf temperature, salinity, and operational stress.

Superior power-to-weight ratios translate to higher fuel efficiency, higher top speeds, and longer operational life — across both leisure and rapid-intervention profiles.
